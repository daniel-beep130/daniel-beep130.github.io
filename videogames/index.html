About the Game

The Last of Us is a survival action-adventure game developed by Naughty Dog. The story follows Joel and Ellie as they travel across a post-apocalyptic United States.

Game Details

  • Release Year: 2013
  • Genre: Action-Adventure / Survival
  • Developer: Naughty Dog
  • Platform: PlayStation
